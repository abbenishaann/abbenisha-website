NutriFix Application (C++)

NutriFix is a C++-based app designed to combat hunger and malnutrition by educating users on nutrition, improving food access, and supporting local farmers. It integrates personalized meal planning, food bank directories, and smallholder farmer marketplaces.

  • Nutritional Education: Offers personalized recommendations and meal plans.
  • Resource Allocation: Maps registered food banks for easy access.
  • Market Access: Enables farmers to sell produce directly via the app.
